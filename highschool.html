My High School Journey

Mission and Vision

Mission: To work together with competent teachers, supportive parents, and the community to mould each unique child towards the total development of his/her multiple intelligence in a caring and nurturing environment.

Vision: Is to develop creative, innovative, and self-propelled learners, often focusing on producing functionally literate, God-loving Filipinos while fostering lifelong learning.
