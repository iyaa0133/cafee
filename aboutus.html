Where It All Began

Kaye's Cafe as a dream of creating a warm and welcoming space filled with comfort and calm. Inspired by dim golden lights, natural elements, and love for coffee, it slowly turned into a place where people can pause, breathe, and enjoy simple moments. What started as an idea became a cozy corner where stories are shared and memories are made.

Meet the Owner

Stephanie Kaye Agustin, a BSIT student, is the heart and soul of Kaye's Cafe. With a love for creativity and a vision of a warm and peaceful environment, she imagined a place where coffee is more than just a drink-it's anexperience. Through her passion and dedication, she created a cafe inspired by cozy lights, nature, and meaningful moments.
